## Pro Tips

1. **Space out submissions** — Don't submit to everything at once. Spread over 4-6 weeks (looks more natural to Google)
2. **Personalize emails** — Generic = 2% response rate. Personal = 15-20% response rate
3. **Add real value** — Guest posts should genuinely help readers, not just promote Resyto
4. **Monitor results** — Use Google Search Console to track which backlinks drive traffic
5. **Follow up** — Wait 5-7 days, then send ONE gentle follow-up if no response
6. **Track in spreadsheet** — Keep record of what you submitted and when
