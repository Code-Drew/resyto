Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

At Resyto, accessible from resyto.com, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information collected and recorded by Resyto and how we use it.

🛡️ Zero File Upload Policy: We do not run any backend processing. All tools, including the Nepali Date Converter and Free Image Compressor, execute entirely within your web browser (client-side). Your images and date entries never leave your device.

1. Information We Collect

Because our applications operate completely client-side in your browser using standard web APIs (such as the Canvas API and JavaScript Date structures), we do not collect, store, or transmit your processed data. Specifically:

  • No File Storage: Photos you select for compression are loaded into your device's memory and processed locally. We do not transmit or store them on any server.
  • No Search Entries Logged: Dates entered for calendar conversions (BS to AD or AD to BS) are processed instantly via offline mathematical calculations in your local browser sandbox.

2. Log Files & Analytics

Like most standard websites, Resyto uses Google Analytics and log files. These files log visitors to the site—usually a standard procedure for hosting platforms and analytics. The information collected by log files includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time stamp of pages visited
  • Referring and exit pages
  • Number of clicks and interactions

This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movement around the site (such as which tools are launched), and gather demographic information. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Resyto uses cookies to store information about visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

4. Advertising Partners & Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to resyto.com and other sites on the internet. Visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Our advertising partners (like Google AdSense) use cookies and web beacons to serve ads directly to your browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content you see on websites.

Note that Resyto has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

5. Third Party Privacy Policies

Resyto's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

6. CCPA & GDPR Data Protection Rights

We want to ensure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. Since we do not collect or store your personal files or application inputs, we hold no user data to delete or modify.
