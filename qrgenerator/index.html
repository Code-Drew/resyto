High-Resolution Vector SVG & PNG Exports

Export your designs in formats optimized for web platforms and commercial printing.

Vector SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics)

SVG files represent images as coordinate paths rather than pixels. This means you can scale the QR code to billboard size or shrink it to a tiny business card without any blurriness or loss of quality. Essential for professional print shops.

Raster PNG (Portable Network Graphics)

PNG is a lossless raster format that supports transparent backgrounds. It is the perfect choice for digital applications, such as website banners, email footers, social media templates, or presentation slides. Compatible with all devices.
